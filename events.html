Jawalakhel Jutta Chappal Byapaar Mela 2024

Welcome to the 3rd Jawalakhel Footwear Business Expo 2024 (Jawalakhel Jutta Chappal Byapaar Mela 2024) ,a one-of-a-kind event showcasing the latest trends and innovations in the world of footwear! Held at the scenic Jawalakhel football ground, this expo brings together a diverse array of stalls featuring shoes from renowned brands, offering visitors an unparalleled shopping experience. From stylish sneakers to elegant dress shoes, there's something for everyone at this premier footwear event. Running from April 22 to May 25, 2024, the Jawalakhel Footwear Business Expo is a must-visit destination for shoe enthusiasts, fashionistas, and industry professionals alike. Explore the latest collections, discover exclusive deals, and interact with representatives from leading footwear companies to stay ahead of the fashion curve. Whether you're in search of the perfect pair for work, play, or special occasions, you'll find it all under one roof at this exciting expo.

Footwear Fiesta

A shoe and sandal trade fair has been organized at four locations in the Kathmandu Valley to mark the New Year 2081. The event, spearheaded by Nepali Shoe House, aims to showcase locally produced footwear and introduce local brands to consumers. The fair, held at Buddha Mall, Balaju, New Road Mall, New Road, Kumaripati, and Satdobato in Lalitpur, was inaugurated by Hemraj Dhakal, the Vice President of the Federation of Commerce and Industry. Homanath Upadhyay, the fair's coordinator, mentioned plans to establish permanent showrooms at all four locations post-event. Notable footwear companies like Royal Shoes, Base Footwear, Cross Road Shoes, First Walk Shoes, DJ Shoes, Run Shoes, Koseli leather shoes, and Smart Shoes are participating, and showcasing their products.
